🐾 Kids & Dog-Friendly Spots

  • San Diego Zoo
    Iconic family-friendly zoo with animals from around the world | ~12 miles | ~$60–70
  • Belmont Park
    Beachfront amusement park with rides, games, and food | ~10 miles | Free entry, rides ~$5–10
  • Balboa Park
    Wide open space with playgrounds, trails, gardens & family museums | ~12 miles | Mostly free
  • Ocean Beach Dog Beach
    Off-leash beach where dogs can play in the surf | ~11 miles | Free
  • Coronado Island Beach
    Calm, scenic family beach – dog-friendly in areas | ~15 miles | Free
  • Liberty Public Market
    Outdoor food market with family & pet-friendly seating areas | ~11 miles | Free entry
